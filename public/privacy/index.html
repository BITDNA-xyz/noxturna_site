Summary

This privacy policy explains how Noxturna ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Noxturna Android app (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

How We Use Information

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share aggregated or anonymized information for analytics. If we use third-party services (such as analytics or distribution), those services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Retention and Security

We retain data as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy and apply reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • Withdraw your consent at any time, as permitted by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us so we can delete it.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page along with the latest update date.
